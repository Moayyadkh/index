The Problem We Solve
 
   
     
Drone companies can't show the future
     
A drone can only film what exists today. For off-plan launches, masterplan presentations, and development pitches — there is nothing to film. Traditional aerial production is useless at the moment it matters most.
   
   
     
Permits, weather, and war risk ground operations
     
GCC airspace is increasingly restricted. Permits take 2–4 weeks. Weather delays compound. Geopolitical uncertainty creates operational risk. Every flight is a logistics problem that can fail at the last moment.
   
   
     
CGI studios work from drawings, not reality
     
Architectural visualization studios produce beautiful renders — but from CAD files and blueprints. Their output doesn't show you real streets, real shadows, real site context. It's a fantasy that floats in white space.
   
   
     
Investors and buyers can't see what they're buying
     
Off-plan real estate in Dubai represents billions in transactions annually. Buyers commit to units they cannot visit, in buildings that don't exist, in neighborhoods they cannot fully understand. Confidence requires vision.
   
 
 
Our Solution
 
   

      Real data.
      Future vision.
      Cinematic output.    

   

      Outcube combines verified satellite imagery from Maxar and Airbus, photogrammetric terrain reconstruction, and AI-powered 3D rendering to produce spatially accurate simulations of developments that haven't been built yet — delivered with the visual quality of a feature film, at a fraction of the cost and time of traditional production.    

 
 
   
      01 / 04            
Satellite Acquisition
     
We source verified imagery of your site from commercial satellite providers at up to 30cm resolution — current, georeferenced, and legally licensed.
     
Maxar · Airbus · Planet Labs · UP42
   
   
      02 / 04       ⬡      
3D Reconstruction
     
Photogrammetric processing builds an accurate terrain mesh — real elevations, real building footprints, real site geometry — from satellite stereo pairs.

AI Development Layer
     
Your development plans, massing, and architecture are placed precisely onto the real site geometry — creating a spatially accurate future-state scene.
     
Unreal Engine 5 · Cinema 4D · AI Enhancement

Cinematic Delivery
     
Photorealistic flythrough video, interactive 3D viewer, or static renders — delivered in 72 hours, ready for launch events, investor decks, and portals.
